About Me

Hello! I’m Yan Ka Man (Aiden Yan), a senior software engineer based in Hong Kong who specialises in Arts Technology, Extended Reality (XR/VR), Educational Technology and Artificial Intelligence. I currently serve as the Senior Software Engineer at the Arts Technology Lab in the Faculty of Arts at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), where I oversee R&D and production of cutting‑edge projects merging technology with the arts.

Over the course of my career I’ve developed virtual reality laboratories for science education, managed GPU clusters for large‑language model research, and designed integrated AIoT solutions for industry partners. My passion is creating immersive experiences that empower learning and creativity.
