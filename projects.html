Projects

Below is a selection of the key projects I’ve led or contributed to across academia and industry. Each entry highlights the purpose, technologies and impact.

VR Anatomy & Animal Dissection System

As part of my role at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), I developed and managed a virtual reality anatomy and animal dissection platform for the course LIFS1904 Laboratory for General Biology. Serving more than 300 life‑science students, the system employs immersive VR to replace physical dissections, offering a scalable and humane alternative for scientific education.

  • Technologies: Unity3D, VR hardware, custom C# scripts
  • Role: Project initiator and technical lead
  • Impact: Increased student engagement and accessibility; reduced reliance on physical specimens

Virtual Laboratory Simulations

I coordinated a suite of Virtual Laboratory Simulations (VLS) for STEM education, preparing students for hands‑on laboratory work through scaffolded, interactive training. The program included A/B testing and data analytics from external developers to continually improve learning outcomes.

  • Technologies: Unity3D, web‑based simulation platforms
  • Role: Project manager and evaluation lead
  • Impact: Delivered quantitative and qualitative data to stakeholders; enhanced student readiness for physical labs

AR/XR Virtual Sets for Education

At the Shaw Auditorium, HKUST, I developed augmented and extended reality virtual sets to enhance teaching and learning experiences. These sets provided immersive backdrops for lectures and performances, blending physical and digital spaces seamlessly.

  • Technologies: Unreal Engine 5, AR/XR hardware, 3D modelling (SketchUp, 3ds Max)
  • Role: Developer and instructor for Technical Theatre Arts
  • Impact: Empowered educators to deliver engaging STEM content with interactive environments

AIoT Solutions & Real‑time Monitoring Platform

As co‑founder of VisuTech Asia Limited, I designed an all‑in‑one AIoT platform integrating customised sensors and a cloud‑based dashboard for real‑time monitoring. Clients included Vitasoy, HK District Cooling, and the Giotto Group.

  • Technologies: Python, Internet‑of‑Things (IoT) hardware, cloud services (Google Cloud)
  • Role: Co‑founder and chief architect
  • Impact: Provided data‑driven insights for industrial partners and built long‑term collaborations

Educational Technology & Digital Law Projects

While consulting for the HKU Faculty of Law, I evaluated and supported digital teaching initiatives for courses on the Rule of Law and Intellectual Property. I supervised the production of MOOCs, designed online examination systems for PCLL students and organised technical support for public seminars.

  • Technologies: Video production, e‑learning platforms, online assessment tools
  • Role: Educational technology consultant
  • Impact: Enabled high‑quality online legal education and streamlined remote assessment

AI & Machine Learning Infrastructure

At HKU’s Arts Technology Lab I oversee GPU servers used for linguistic and machine‑learning research. Projects include fine‑tuning large language models for analysing Cantonese corpora and providing computational resources for arts researchers.

  • Technologies: Python, PyTorch, large‑language models, GPU cluster administration
  • Role: Systems administrator and ML engineer
  • Impact: Facilitated cutting‑edge research in computational linguistics and creative AI
